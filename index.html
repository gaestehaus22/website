Apartment with single beds and complete equipment for workers and business travelers!

Gästehaus 22 specializes in the needs of companies and their employees. Thanks to the proximity to the A1 motorway (exit Asten/St. Florian), you can reach the industrial areas in Linz, Enns, Steyr, and St. Valentin within a few minutes.

Our accommodation offers everything workers need for long-term or short-term stays: stable single beds, a fully equipped shared kitchen, and high-speed Wi-Fi for your evening.
